Security Guard – Missionary Training Center

Sep 2024 – Jun 2025 • Provo, UT

Overview

Served as a Security Guard at the Missionary Training Center, responsible for maintaining safety and security across a large, multi-building campus. The role required constant situational awareness, accurate documentation, and effective coordination with team members in a high-responsibility environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitored and operated four networked surveillance systems covering 21 buildings, ensuring continuous visibility across the campus.
  • Used real-time video monitoring tools, analytics, and alert systems to identify and respond to suspicious or unusual activity.
  • Documented incidents and daily activity through detailed digital logs and formal incident reports.
  • Coordinated with other security personnel to respond to incidents and maintain consistent security coverage.

Skills & Competencies

Security operations, surveillance systems, incident reporting, attention to detail, teamwork, situational awareness
