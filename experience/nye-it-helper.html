Summer IT Helper – Nye County School District

Jun 2025 – Aug 2025 • Pahrump, NV

Overview

Supported district-wide IT operations for Nye County School District during a summer infrastructure upgrade period. Worked alongside full-time IT staff to deploy new network equipment, maintain systems, and ensure continuity of services across multiple school sites.

Key Responsibilities

  • Deployed and configured over 15 network switches across district locations, ensuring proper connectivity and integration with existing network architecture.
  • Integrated new networking hardware with legacy infrastructure, troubleshooting compatibility issues and minimizing downtime during upgrades.
  • Assisted with system administration tasks including daily backups, user account management, and routine hardware and software troubleshooting.
  • Documented IT workflows and created step-by-step support guides to improve onboarding efficiency and knowledge transfer for future staff and interns.

Technical Skills & Tools

Networking fundamentals, system administration, backups, user account management, technical documentation, troubleshooting
