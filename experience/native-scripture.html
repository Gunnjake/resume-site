Native Scripture – On Campus Internship

Jan 2025 – May 2025 • Provo, UT

Overview

Native Scripture is a scripture publishing company focused on producing dual-language scripture resources to support language learning and accessibility. During this on-campus internship, I worked as part of a small project team supporting strategic, research, and technical initiatives in collaboration with organizational sponsors.

Key Responsibilities

  • Met weekly with a cross-functional student team to plan project milestones, coordinate tasks, and review progress with Native Scripture sponsors.
  • Researched and compiled a list of potential influencers and partners aligned with Native Scripture’s mission, then ranked candidates based on reach, relevance, and collaboration potential.
  • Supported outreach efforts by organizing contact information and assisting with structured communication strategies for partnership and collaboration requests.
  • Conducted research on scripture publishing rights and licensing considerations across different languages to support international expansion and compliance.
  • Built an automated email reply workflow using n8n to streamline responses and improve efficiency in handling inbound communications.

Technical & Professional Skills Applied

Workflow automation (n8n), research and analysis, stakeholder communication, documentation, project planning, team collaboration
