Volunteer Representative — Philippines

Jun 2022 – Jun 2024 • Legazpi, Philippines

Served as a full-time volunteer representative in the Philippines, focusing on teaching, mentoring, and community service. The role required strong leadership, cross-cultural communication, and adaptability while working closely with local communities.

  • Taught and mentored 10+ individuals daily in both Tagalog and English, adapting instruction to diverse cultural and educational backgrounds.
  • Directed and supported a team of 12 volunteer representatives, providing leadership, training, and performance feedback.
  • Led and participated in landscaping and cleanup service projects benefiting over 500 students and community members.
  • Lived with and trained groups of up to 8 volunteers at a time, providing ongoing instruction, coordination, and support.
